    • Back to School Block Party Campaign

    In Collaboration with Phenixx Marketing

    Project Overview:


    The Tyrese Maxey Foundation's Back to School Block Party campaign aimed to achieve remarkable success by effectively promoting a community event. The campaign's overarching mission was to empower Garland school children by providing essential supplies and services, including backpacks, school supplies, laptops, free haircuts, and food options for local families through collaboration with partners.

    Roles and Responsibilities:


    • Collaborate on the content strategy for the campaign.
    • Evaluate social media analytics for a better understanding of content performance.
    • Provide insights to enhance future campaigns based on analytics data.

    Challenges and Solutions:


      Challenges:
    • The need to inform the audience about event details and encourage participation.
    • The goal of raising funds and securing collaboration partners for the event.
      Solutions:
    • Developed a comprehensive content strategy involving template-based posts to highlight enticing features.
    • Utilized a blend of content formats, including static posts, carousels, reels, and stories.
    • Evaluated social media analytics for a data-driven understanding of content performance.

    Results and Impact:


    • Raised $30,000 in donations and secured ten partner collaborations.
    • Reached 23,355 individuals and achieved a 10% engagement rate on Instagram.
    • Shared 68 content pieces across various formats, leading to 2,355 interactions.
    View Campaign Report

    This impactful campaign exemplifies the power of collaborative content strategy and data-driven decision-making in fostering positive change, showcasing the success of our approach in promoting and supporting a meaningful community event.
