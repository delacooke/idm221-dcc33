    • Lubbock Juneteenth

    In Collaboration with Phenixx Marketing

    Project Overview:

    In a collaborative effort with Phenixx Marketing, this project aimed to create extensive brand awareness, drive event participation, and attract sponsors, vendors, and volunteers Rise Up Weekend 2023, a Juneteenth celebration event. The project focused on positioning the event as a cultural celebration through strategic planning and execution.

    Roles and Responsibilities:


    In this project, my roles and responsibilities included:

    • Devising a comprehensive strategy for Lubbock Juneteenth.
    • Developing and implementing a strategic content calendar.
    • Collaborating on omni-channel promotion to expand reach and engagement.
    • Strategically driving traffic to the event's website for increased sponsorships and ticket sales.


    Challenges and Solutions:


      Challenges:

    • Limited brand awareness and event participation.
    • Need to attract sponsors, vendors, and volunteers.

      • Solutions:

    • Implemented a comprehensive strategy with a strategic content calendar.
    • Executed omni-channel promotion for expanded reach.
    • Focused on driving traffic to the event's website for increased support.


    Results and Impact:

    • Lubbock Juneteenth's Rise Up Weekend achieved record-breaking attendance.
    • Instagram metrics demonstrated an average reach rate increase of over 200%.
    • Engagement rate on Instagram soared to an impressive 12.3%.
    • Efforts contributed to a remarkable 105% increase in website traffic compared to the previous year.


    These results solidified Lubbock Juneteenth's status as a vibrant cultural celebration, showcasing the success of our strategic marketing approach in driving significant growth and engagement.
